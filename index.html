One ecosystem, end to end

Every CIIFA programme feeds the next. Talent discovers a path, builds verified skills, turns skill into enterprise, and meets the market — and the ecosystem compounds.

1 · Discover & learn

The Skill Pool and CBA for Africa give talent a structured route from curiosity to competence to creative business mastery.

2 · Build & grow

The Enterprise Programme turns working creatives into company builders, with deliverables that investors and partners can act on.

3 · Connect & earn

The Career Fair and the SafeHouse connect talent to employers, opportunity and a community that holds the door open.
