The Creative Career Fair

Talent on one side, opportunity on the other, and no velvet rope in between. The room where Africa's creative careers actually start.

2026 · CBA Week — week of 23 Nov 2027 · returns standalone in July Employers · studios · labels · brands

What it is

The Creative Career Fair connects creative talent with the organisations that need them — employers, studios, labels, agencies and brands. Unlike a conventional job fair, the talent in the room comes with verified skills and real deliverables: Skill Pool badges, CBA cohort work and Enterprise Programme outputs, not just CVs.

The editions

  • 2026: the Fair runs inside CBA for Africa Week (week of 23 November) — graduation week, when the year's best new talent and the industry are already in the same city.
  • 2027: the Fair returns as a standalone mid-year event in July, then anchors CBA Week again in November via the AMEDI Festival.

For employers

If you hire creative talent — designers, editors, producers, marketers, managers — the Fair is the shortest path to people who can already do the work. Reserve interest as a hiring partner →
