The Skill Pool Initiative

A ladder, not a lottery. Six levels from first-time explorer to investor — so every creative in Africa can see exactly where they are and what's next.

6 levels · L0 Explorer → L5 Investor 3 tracks 6 sectors 108+ pathways Verified badges

What it is

The Skill Pool is the talent infrastructure underneath everything CIIFA does. It maps the creative industries into six sectors, three tracks and six levels — over 108 learning pathways — so that skills are structured, verifiable and portable across the whole ecosystem.

  • Start free at L0. The Learn Hub opens the door with 130+ curated free resources, in four languages, Africa-first.
  • Climb with badges. Every level earned is a verified badge employers and programmes can trust.
  • Careers, mapped. 45 sub-industries and 127 career ladders show the real routes from learning to earning.

Where it leads

Skill Pool levels plug directly into the rest of the ecosystem: badge-holders surface at the Creative Career Fair, feed into CBA for Africa and the Enterprise Programme, and build a profile that travels with them.

The Skill Pool platform is live in preview. The public platform link will be connected here at launch — enquiries: info@ciif.africa.
