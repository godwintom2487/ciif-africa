The CIIFA SafeHouse

Where creativity meets comfort. A creative hub in Ikoyi, Lagos offering state-of-the-art studios, comfortable accommodation, and inspiring event spaces.

The facilities

Everything a creative needs to record, stay, meet and host — in one compound at 4B Onikoyi Lane, Parkview Estate, Ikoyi.

Record

Recording Studios

Three professional studios (capacity 6 each) with the latest recording equipment, a professional engineer included in every session, mixing & mastering available, and a comfortable lounge area.

₦172,000 per 4-hour session · engineer included
Stay

Accommodation

Standard and master rooms for individuals and groups (2–5 guests) — clean linens, air conditioning and WiFi throughout; master rooms add ensuite bathrooms and premium linens.

From ₦40,000 per night · standard ₦40,000 · single master ₦50,000 · group master ₦60,000
Host

Event Space

A lawn space for up to 40 guests with a bar — listening sessions, showcases, screenings, launches and private gatherings under the Lagos sky.

₦400,000 per 8 hours
Meet

Meeting Room

A professional meeting space for up to 8 people — for writing camps, label meetings, pitches and planning sessions.

₦25,000 per hour

More than a facility

The SafeHouse is the community heart of the CIIFA ecosystem — the home base where the people behind the work record, rest, meet and connect. Skill Pool talent, CBA cohorts and Enterprise Programme graduates all pass through its doors.
