You bring the industry. We bring the machine.

CIIFA runs the curriculum, admissions, platform and operations. Partners bring reach, faculty, venues, jobs and capital. Together that's an industry.

Ways in

Four ways organisations work with CIIFA today.

Delivery partners

Co-deliver a programme

Industry organisations co-deliver Enterprise Programme bootcamps in their vertical — contributing faculty, space and credibility on a revenue-share basis, with co-billing and first right on the next funded edition.

Sponsors

Sponsor a cohort or a moment

Put your brand behind a CBA cohort, a Career Fair, or a showcase — visible support for creative talent at the moments the whole industry is watching.

Employers

Hire verified talent

Recruit at the Creative Career Fair and from the Skill Pool — talent with verified skills, real deliverables and badge-backed profiles, not just CVs.

Institutions & government

Build infrastructure with us

Development agencies, cultural institutions and governments partner with CIIFA and CTF to scale creative-economy programmes across markets.
